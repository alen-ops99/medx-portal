Program Overview

The Med&X Accelerator places exceptional students and early-career researchers at prestigious institutions including Harvard, Yale, MIT, Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, and MGH. Gain hands-on research experience and mentorship from world-leading scientists.
